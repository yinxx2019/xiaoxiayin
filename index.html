    tech savvy

    I still feel the warmth of Summer 2018 in Seattle, when I saw the sunshine came through the reflective windows at Russell Investments Center. What warmed me was not only the sunshine: my public relations internship experience at Zillow Group sparked my interest in exploring a career that balances my passion for mass communications and technology.

    After I graduated at Washington and Lee University with a BA degree in Strategic Communication and Politics, I was lucky enough to become part of the CASE Advancement Internship Program. California Institute of Technology's Advancement Information Services Department hosted me as a Project Management Intern. This experience working on communication and marketing in a tech setting further developed my interest in gaining more technical skills.

    As of 2020, I'm pursuing a MS degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University - Seattle. As I enjoy my new journey in coding, web development and software engineering, I'm looking forward to bringing my liberal arts background and technical skills to a job function that benefits the technology industry and our society.
