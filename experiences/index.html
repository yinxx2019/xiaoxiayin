Zillow Group | Public Relations Intern | Summer 2018 | Seattle

Company Profile

Zillow Group is an online real estate database company founded in 2006. Zillow Group CEO Spencer Rascoff also called Zillow a “real estate media company” because it has “all this incredible information” and “it empowers consumers.” . Zillow’s headquarter is in downtown Seattle with over 2000 employees.

My Duties

Daily coverage monitoring and coverage report

I monitored Zillow, ZG and competitors’ coverage and wrote a coverage report by the end of day. I filtered all coverage in the tracker. I checked if the reporters have any factually incorrect statements on Zillow and flagged coverage to appropriate team members. I uploaded the coverage report to the internal website and sent it to the executive team, communication team and ZG’s consulting firm. At the end of each month, I wrote a monthly roundup, which included highlights of the months and number counts of each category of news in the trackers.

Media lists

I created and updated around 20 media lists by metros and beats. I used Cision for the first-round research, and I briefly went through the stories of reporters to make sure we found the right contact.

Pitching and coordinating interviews

My metros were Sacramento, Calif. and Columbus, Ohio. I usually pitched twice a week, using bullet points for factual information found by Zillow economists’ researches. I received interview requests from reporters sometimes, so I needed to coordinate between economists and reports to schedule interviews via phone or Skype, depended on the nature of the outlet. At the last week, I also got an interview request from a large Chinese TV station, so I took advantage of my language ability.

Press Release

I pulled data from a spreadsheet with housing data from 1999 to 2018 and the forecast rate when writing the news release about the “Million Dollars Cities.” I used Excel to figure out which cities are forecasted to have million dollars median home prices in the next 12 months. I also pulled the list of current “ Million Dollars Cities” by Excel for comparison. I analyzed the data and “translated what the data means for homeowners, homebuyers, renters and people in certain cities. I did markets researches on two cities in a comparatively “cold” housing markets forecasted to have million dollars home prices.

Canadian real estate coverage and competitors analysis

I monitored Canadian news coverage every day and analyzed the styles, preferences and topics of all real estate, personal finance and lifestyle reporters. In addition, I evaluated the coverage pattern of all major Canadian outlets and developed pitch strategies for each. I also researched potential Zillow competitors in Canada, analyzed their coverage and gave suggestions on what Zillow should do to stand out. I’ m proud that the strategies I created worked very well and my research helped a lot when the company entered the Canadian market.

“Office Hours”

Zillow Group CEO Spencer Rascoff has his own program called “Office Hours,” in which he did interviews with famous journalists, entrepreneurs and CEOs of startups and put the interview recordings public for anyone needs inspirations. However, since he is very busy most of the time, the storytelling team is in charge of providing the background and briefing documents of each interviewee he is going to talk to. It is a fun project involves in research, finding the best angle and creative writing. I wrote three background documents for “Office Hours.”

China Everbright Limited | Communications Intern | Spring 2017 | Hong Kong, China

Company Profile

China Everbright Limited is a cross-border financial services company in asset management, investment and merchant bank. It manages private equity funds, venture capital funds, sector focus funds, mezzanine funds, fund of funds, fixed income and equity funds. It is a Hong Kong-based company and one of the biggest cross-border investment companies in Greater China area.

My Duties

Event Planning

I helped organize an investment and insurance themed forum featuring more than 10 renowned experts and practitioners of the insurance industry in China. About 200 companies in the insurance and asset management industry would send representatives to participate in the forum. I kept the records and information of each participant, went to the conference centers to help decided the conference rooms holding the event, coordinated with the co-organizers and planning logistics and materials. I was also in charge of the reception and logistics.

Press releases and internal publications

I wrote the press release and internal report of the forum in Chinese and English. I wrote articles for the internal publication named “Fellow.” The theme for the issue is “sport and entrepreneurship” and the section I’m writing for are the “Brand Story” and “Different trends” section. I brainstorm and researched the proper topics to motivate employees in the mainland and Hong Kong to understand the corporate culture and each other.

Media Relations

I tracked the press interview of the CEO of CEL from four news media with different categories: TV, radio, newspaper and website. I communicated and coordinated with the reporters, logistics staff and CEO himself to prepare for the interview. Tracking the interview is a good experience to see the different requirements of the interviews from different kinds of news media.

Social Media Research and Strategy

I conducted a research project on improving the use of social media in CEL. I did the case study, analyzing the advantages and disadvantages of certain social media domestically and internationally. I analyzed the current situation of the use of CEL social media and compared to the use of social media of main competitors of CEL to propose the methods to improve it in internal and external communication. I wrote a report in traditional Chinese and gave a presentation to the colleagues in Hong Kong and Beijing. There are three major benefits I brought to the company by my research: first, I improved the publicity of the current WeChat (China’s major social media) account of CEL by rearranging the page and improving searching engine optimization. Secondly, I helped create a new LinkedIn account of CEL for external communication. I also developed an effective method of using WeChat for internal communication.
